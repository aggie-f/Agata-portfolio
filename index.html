About me

Hello, my name is Agata and welcome to my website. I am Polish, moved to London after graduating from Uni where I studied economics and management. Living in London in your early twenties can take an unexpected course, and this also what happened to me. I had an amazing opportunity to work for 13 years directly with the same managing director creating and managing very successful and award-winning business, Meat (Butcher & Delicatessen). During this time, we opened 3 successful shops, run a number of events and developed and managed some wonderful and talented people. Last year I decided to change my career and become a Web Developer. I am at the moment of my professional life where I am consciously in need of new challenges, being more creative and learning new skills. I find it fascinating to learn how to code and experience new technologies and enjoy very much working on various projects with other people and creating and developing new ideas. I like to keep myself busy outside work as well as to keep my life in balance. I am regularly practicing Tai Chi and Chinese calligraphy and love Asian culture and cuisine. I also like to go a bit wild during live gigs and travel around the world, neither of which I can do much of these days unfortunately.. I studied wine with WSET, and love to enjoy a good glass of wine with friends on occasions. Recently I developed green fingers and love working in my garden!
